## Summary — biggest power flows

**Largest by capacity (MW/GW):**
1. **Switch → Oklo, 12 GW** SMR master agreement (non-binding) — biggest single MW number but a framework, no dollar value, first reactor not yet operating.
2. **AEP utility load: 24 GW committed by 2030 (18 GW data centers)** — the largest *signed* committed-load figure (ERCOT/PJM/SPP).
3. **Oracle → Bloom Energy, up to 2.8 GW** SOFC fuel cells (1.2 GW contracted) — the marquee on-site/behind-the-meter equipment deal, Apr 2026.

**Largest by disclosed dollars:**
1. **Amazon → Talen, ~$18B / 17 yrs** for up to 1,920 MW of Susquehanna nuclear — the clearest hard-$ PPA (SEC 8-K confirmed). ✅ Confirmed the ~$18B / 1,920 MW / 17-yr figures you asked to verify.
2. **KKR/Nvidia/KIA Helix → Vistra, >$10B** committed capital with Vistra as preferred power provider (Jun 2026).
3. Corporate context: **Constellation–Calpine $16.4B equity / ~$26.6B EV** acquisition (Jan 2026) is the largest balance-sheet move reshaping the AI-power supply side (not itself a DC→power flow).

**Nuclear PPA cluster (hyperscaler → reactor owner):** Microsoft→Constellation Crane 835 MW/20-yr; Meta→Constellation Clinton 1,121 MW/20-yr; Amazon+Meta→Vistra ~3.8 GW; Google→Kairos 500 MW; Meta→Oklo 1.2 GW.

### What I could NOT source / gaps to flag
- **Dollar values of most nuclear PPAs are undisclosed.** Only the Amazon–Talen deal has an official ~$18B figure. Microsoft–Constellation (Crane), Meta–Constellation (Clinton), Vistra's AWS/Meta PPAs, and Google–Kairos all report MW/term but **no contract $**. Any dollar figure for these would be an estimate, not sourced.
- **Oracle's three-SMR ~1 GW data center:** the SMR developer/vendor is **not named** and there is **no dollar figure or firm timeline** — disclosed only via Ellison's earnings-call remarks; treat as directional.
- **Oklo pipeline (Switch 12 GW, Meta 1.2 GW, Equinix 500 MW):** all **non-binding framework agreements/LOIs**; only the Equinix LOI has an attached figure ($25M prepayment). Not firm offtake.
- **GE Vernova / Eaton DC figures are aggregate backlog/order metrics**, not customer-specific edges — I could not source individual hyperscaler→GEV or →Eaton named contracts with MW + $.
- **Bloom–Oracle 2.8 GW: dollar value undisclosed** (a share warrant is attached but not a contract price).
- **Entergy / Dominion specific DC PPAs:** found aggregate generation-buildout figures (Entergy ~4.5 GW for Google/Meta) but **no clean per-deal MW + $**; Dominion did not surface a marquee single deal in this pass — a gap if needed.
- Several figures lean on secondary aggregators (Luminix, EnkiAI, TIKR); the marquee deals above are corroborated by primary/Tier-1 sources (SEC, company IR, Utility Dive, PowerMag, DCD), but the Vistra 3.8 GW breakdown specifically should be re-confirmed against Vistra's own 10-Q/earnings deck before publication.
