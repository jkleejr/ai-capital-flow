## Notes & Caveats

- **Nvidia → CoreWeave**: Two distinct things — (a) the equity stake (passively held public shares, ~11.5% of Class A per 13G/A; reported ~13% overall including pre-IPO) and (b) the separate **$6.3B capacity backstop** (Nvidia as buyer of last resort through 2032). The equity dollar value moves with share price; the latest 13F-based figure is ~$3.66B. Treat the "$2B in January" framing (TechCrunch/CNBC) and the "~$2B added in Q4 2025" framing as overlapping reporting of Nvidia's share accumulation.
- **Nvidia → OpenAI**: This is the most fluid number. The Sept 2025 "$100B" was an explicit **letter of intent**, milestone-linked, and Nvidia's CFO publicly called it "not definitive" as late as Dec 2025. Reporting from Feb 2026 describes a restructured **~$30B equity stake** within OpenAI's ~$110B round. As of mid-2026 the $30B is widely reported but I could not confirm a clean "fully funded/closed" primary disclosure — flag as announced, not confirmed-closed.
- **Google → Anthropic "$40B closed March 2026"**: I could NOT confirm a close in March 2026. Sources date the "up to $40B" commitment to **April 2026**, with $10B immediate cash (at $350B valuation) and $30B contingent. If your timeline says "closed March 2026," that appears inaccurate or refers to a different/earlier tranche — recommend revising to "announced April 2026, staged."
- **Amazon → Anthropic total**: ~$8B historical is confirmed. The new "up to $25B" (April 2026) is a fresh commitment on top, so cumulative committed is materially higher than $8B going forward, but only ~$8B + an initial $5B is near-term funded.
